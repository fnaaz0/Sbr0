Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Welcome to SBR Super App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

A. Location Data

Our app requires access to your device's precise geographical location to automatically calculate and display the accurate Islamic prayer times (Salah times) based on your current region. This location data is processed locally on your device or via secure real-time API requests. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location data to any external third-party servers, nor do we track your location history.

B. Chat Room Data

Our real-time chat room functionality utilizes Firebase services. The text messages and user identifiers sent within the chat room are processed solely to deliver real-time communication between active users. By using the chat room, you acknowledge that your messages are visible to other users in the public room.

2. User-Generated Content (UGC) Policy

As a provider of interactive chat features, we strictly enforce a zero-tolerance policy for objectionable content or abusive behavior. Users have the right to report inappropriate messages or block abusive participants within the system interface. Any reported content will be reviewed promptly, and violating users will be banned from the platform.

3. External Payment and Portfolio Links

Our app provides redirect links to external platforms such as Gumroad to showcase digital calligraphy art. This app does not feature native digital sales or in-app billing. All external browsing and external interactions are subject to the terms and privacy policies of those respective third-party platforms.

4. Security

We implement industry-standard administrative and technical security measures to safeguard your app interactions. However, no electronic transmission over the internet can be guaranteed 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact the developer team through our official GitHub repository interface.
